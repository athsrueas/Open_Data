Information on the Common Core of Data (CCD)

The primary purpose of the CCD is to provide basic information on public elementary and secondary schools, local education agencies (LEAs), and state education agencies (SEAs) for each state, the District of Columbia, and the outlying territories with a U.S. relationship.

CCD is composed of two components: Nonfiscal CCD and Fiscal CCD. To see what years are available for each component, click here:

The following information is provided by Nonfiscal CCD:

  • Directory data (schools, LEAs, SEAs)
  • State-level student membership counts disaggregated by grade, race/ethnicity and sex (schools, LEAs, SEAs)
  • Teacher FTE counts (schools, LEAs, SEAs)
  • Staff FTE counts by professional category (LEAs, SEAs)
  • Counts of Children with disabilities and English learners (LEA)
  • Counts of students eligible for free and reduced-price lunches (school)

If you have any questions concerning Nonfiscal CCD, please contact the EDFacts team.

The following information is provided by Fiscal CCD:

  • Revenues (local, state and federal sources)
  • Expenditures by function and object
  • General administration, School administration, Operations and maintenance, and Facilities acquisition and construction expenditures)
  • Average Daily attendance (SEA level)
  • Student membership (LEA and SEA level)

The fiscal year (FY) is the 12-month period to which the annual operating budget applies. For example, FY18 would correspond to the 2017-18 school year.

If you have any questions for Fiscal CCD, please contact Stephen Cornman or Patrick Keaton.

Hint: Working with the raw data files can be tedious and time-consuming. Some data file sizes, especially for student membership data, may be over 1GB. You may find it easier to get the data you need from the Table Generator. The table generator provides access to all the years, levels and components of CCD data in an easy-to-use process you control. No programming required.

For nonfiscal CCD, refer to the Online Documentation for more information about how the CCD data are collected and processed. Documentation of data issues spanning multiple years, levels or components of CCD is available in the CCD Reference Library. For questions or concerns regarding nonfiscal CCD, please email EDFacts Partner Support Center (EDFacts@ed.gov).

NCES forecasts the release of statistics and statistical products in the Statistical Products Release Calendar.
